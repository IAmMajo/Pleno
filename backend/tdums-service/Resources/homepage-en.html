Disclosure

Thank you for visiting our website!

We would like to inform you that the campaign "Weihnachtswnsche" was created as part of a scientific experiment. Everything related to "Weihnachtswnsche" is purely fictional and made up! The goal of the experiment was to demonstrate the mechanisms and risks of social engineering in a playful way and raise awareness of the importance of cyber security.

The content of this website, as well as the contest, were specifically designed for this experiment and have no real background. No data was collected or processed, and the experiment was completely harmless for all participants.

For more information on social engineering and how you can protect yourself, we recommend the following sources:

  • BSI: Social Engineering the "Human Factor"
  • Kaspersky: What is Social Engineering?
  • Kaspersky: Social Engineering Protection & Prevention
  • Wikipedia: Social Engineering (security)

We thank you for your participation and hope that this experiment has helped you better understand the dangers and techniques of social engineering.
